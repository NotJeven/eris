About

In this asymmetrical battle, players are divided into invading and defending teams. The Invaders objective is to destroy the redstone core before it becomes fully charged. The core will take 15 minutes to charge, and once it is fully charged, the Defenders will win!

After a match has ended, use the in map admin options to swap sides! Try to defend longer than your opponent, or mount a faster invasion.
